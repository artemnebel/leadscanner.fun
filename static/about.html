> ABOUT.EXE

> WHAT_IS_LEAD_SCANNER

Lead Scanner is a free tool built for freelance web designers and agencies who want to find local businesses that don't have a website. Instead of guessing who needs your services, you can search any city, drag a radius on the map, and instantly see every business in that area with no online presence.

Every result comes with a phone number, rating, review count, and a direct link to their Google Maps profile — everything you need to start your outreach.

> WHY_IT_WAS_BUILT

Finding web design clients is one of the hardest parts of running a freelance business. Cold outreach works, but only when you're reaching out to businesses that actually need a website. Lead Scanner solves the discovery problem — it filters out businesses that already have an online presence and hands you a clean list of genuine prospects.

Lead Scanner is currently free to use. As the tool grows, paid plans with expanded features may be introduced — but early users will always be the first to know.

> HOW_IT_WORKS_UNDER_THE_HOOD

Lead Scanner is powered by the Google Places API. When you run a scan, it searches for businesses matching your category within the radius you set. It then checks each business for a website field — if a website is present, the business is skipped. Only businesses with a phone number but no website make it into your leads list.

The backend is built with Python (FastAPI) and the frontend uses vanilla JavaScript with Leaflet.js for the interactive map. The full source code is available on GitHub.

> WHO_BUILT_IT

Lead Scanner was built as a personal tool and open-sourced for anyone in web design or digital marketing who wants a faster way to find clients. Have feedback or ideas? Use the contact page.
