> WEBSITE_BUILDERS_TO_OFFER_CLIENTS

When building sites for local businesses, these platforms let you deliver fast results at low cost:

  • Framer — best for modern, design-forward sites. Fast to build, looks premium.
  • Webflow — full control over design, great for custom builds. Steeper learning curve.
  • Squarespace — easiest to hand off to clients for self-management. Great templates.
  • WordPress — most flexible, huge plugin ecosystem. Good for SEO-heavy sites.
  • Wix — fast and client-friendly. Works well for restaurants and service businesses.

> PRICING_GUIDANCE

Typical pricing for local business websites (one-time cost):

  • Starter (1-page site): $300 – $700 — hours, location, contact form, Google Maps embed
  • Standard (3-5 pages): $700 – $1,500 — home, about, services, gallery, contact
  • Premium (custom): $1,500+ — booking systems, e-commerce, SEO optimization

Don't underprice. Small businesses expect to pay for professional services. Charging too little signals low quality.
