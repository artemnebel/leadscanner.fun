> FAQ.TXT
What is Lead Scanner?

Lead Scanner finds local businesses without a website. Search any city by category, set a radius on the map, and get a list of businesses with a phone number but no website so you can pitch your web design services.

How does it find businesses without a website?

It uses the Google Places API in real time. Every scan pulls fresh data, checks each result for a website field, and only returns businesses that have a phone number but no website.

Is Lead Scanner free to use?

Yes. The free plan includes 100 leads per month with no credit card required. Paid plans start at $29/month for higher limits.

What information does each lead include?

Business name, phone number, city, Google rating, review count, and a direct link to their Google Maps profile.

Can I export my leads?

Yes. Export all leads as CSV, JSON, or XLSX with one click. Every export includes phone numbers and Maps links.

What business categories work with Lead Scanner?

Any category you can search on Google Maps works: plumbers, restaurants, hair salons, dentists, landscapers, roofers, auto repair, and more.
